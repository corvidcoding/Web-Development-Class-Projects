About Camper's Trail

We created this site to share our love and passion for camping. When it comes to learning all sorts of skills and knowledge in camping, you'll find that all sorts of information is scattered in videos, random articles and books or word of mouth. This can quite intimidating for new and unexperienced campers. So we decided it would be neat if there was a place where all sorts of camping knowledge and resources be available in one place, hence this site was created.

Of course, as a hub for resources we want this place to be valuable not just to newcomers but welcoming to seasoned campers as well. This site focuses on resources that covers topics for all skill levels and we're open to any contributions you like to share.

To be the the ultimate camping resource, this is a site that's build by campers, for campers.
